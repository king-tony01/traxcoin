What Is TraxCoin

TraxCoin enables the creation and composition of TraxCoin Tokens. Where traditional tokens function as assets and proof of ownership, TraxCoin Tokens function as mini dApps carrying business logic, and a programmable interface, enabling tokens to become composable and modular applications opening up a new world of tokenized use cases.
