ABOUT US

Traxcoin is a launchpad powered by the TraxNYC corp. which was founded in 2003 by its current president , Maksud Trax Agadjani.The Traxcoin decentralizes the routing layer and makes Web3 and peer-to-peer communications truly peer-to-peer and fully programmable using smart contracts, allowing new and innovative projects to seamlessly prepare for launch with an emphasis on fair and broad distribution with its values deeply rooted in the early TraxNYC community.
